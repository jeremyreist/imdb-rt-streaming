Adds IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes ratings to Netflix titles.

Simply enable the extension and hover over any title to see the ratings.
Very easy on CPU and memory, this lightweight extension adds IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes ratings to every title available.

Features:
    -    Displays IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes ratings on every title card.
    -    Settings panel to enable or disable all features of extension.
    -    Color options can be toggled.
